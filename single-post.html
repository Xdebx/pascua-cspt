Computer is an electronic device that is capable of processing data, performing calculations, and making logical decisions based on instructions provided by a user. It is composed of hardware components such as a central processing unit (CPU), memory, storage, and input/output devices, and software, which is a set of instructions that tell the computer what to do. Computers are used in a wide range of applications, including personal and business computing, scientific simulations, and multimedia production. They have become an essential tool in modern society and have revolutionized many aspects of our lives.

The Kingston 250GB NV1 NVMe PCIe M.2 is a solid-state drive (SSD) with a capacity of 250GB and uses the NVMe protocol over a PCIe interface. The drive is M.2 form factor, which is a compact and efficient design commonly used in modern laptops and desktop computers. NVMe is a high-performance storage protocol that provides faster read and write speeds compared to traditional SATA-based SSDs. The Seagate 1TB SATA is a traditional hard disk drive (HDD) with a capacity of 1TB and uses the SATA III interface to connect to the computer. SATA III provides a fast data transfer rate of 6Gbps, but it is slower compared to NVMe-based SSDs. The 1TB capacity provides ample storage for everyday use, but the drive may be slower to access data compared to an NVMe SSD.

The 80+ efficiency rating indicates that it is able to deliver at least 80% of its rated output power as useful power to your system, with the remaining power lost as heat. The ES230 model features active PFC (power factor correction) for improved electrical performance and energy efficiency.
